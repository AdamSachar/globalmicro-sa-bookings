📋 Mom’s Till Slips

A simple app to add up till slips for the catering.

📷 Point the phone’s camera at this square, then tap the link that pops up.

adamsachar.github.io/globalmicro-sa-bookings/catering/

📱 On an Android phone (Chrome)

  1. Open the link above (or scan the square).
  2. Tap the big “Install app on this phone” button.
  3. An icon appears on the home screen — tap it any time.

🍎 On an iPhone (Safari)

  1. Open the link above in Safari.
  2. Tap Share ⬆️, then “Add to Home Screen”.
  3. An icon appears on the home screen — tap it any time.

✅ How Mom uses it

  1. Tap + New Job and name the event.
  2. Tap + Add Till Slip — the camera opens. Photograph the slip and the total is read in for her. She checks it and taps Save.
  3. The total adds up by itself. Tap Export / Share to send it to you.
